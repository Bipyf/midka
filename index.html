To the official website of Cryptid Hunting Association

About us:

The Cryptid Hunting Association is a non-governmental organization that is specified in hunting cryptids. The Hunting Association consists of hundreds of skilled hunters from all over the world. Our duty is to hunt down and eliminate most dangerous creatures in the world. We work day and night to fight cryptids so that humanity won't live in fear. We started operating as an organization since 1787 - when the team of hunters led by Erich Linaeus successfully tracked down and killed the legendary "Kraken". Few years later Erich Linaeus have opened a Foundation of Demonhunting and started recruiting more hunters and soldiers for the cause of Cryptid hunting. A century later during industrial revolution the Foundation of Demonhunting reformes into CHA as we know it.



What are Cryptids?

The Cryptids are unnatural creatures that may directly or undirectly cause a threat to mankind. These creatures are not only physiologically superior but also very unpredictable. No one knows where they came from but we know for sure that these creatures even existed long before humans. To give you the idea let's talk about one of the most popular cryptid out there. The Loch-Ness monster is a huge reptile that inhabitates the Loch-Ness lake in the Scotland. You may have heard about it in the internet or movies. Fortunatelt for us this creature is currently detained and is under our cutody for research purposes.

How can you help?

Our organization is constantly on lookout for a talented hunters. But if you have strong background on biology, you can be enlisted as one of our researchers. If you're willing to sacrifice yourself for humanity's wellbeing then you can apply for a membership in this website. If you have seen any suspicous activity or have witnessed a cryptid disturbing your place, you can report what you saw on this website and we will try to answer as quickly as we can.
